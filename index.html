CHOOSE & ENJOY

Whether you crave traditional Middle Eastern flavors or bold modern twists, this is where your shawarma adventure begins. Indulge your cravings and savor every bite as you create your personalized wrap masterpiece with Shawarma House.

Classic shawarma

The timeless favorite. Tender marinated chicken or beef, slowly grilled on a rotating spit, thinly sliced and wrapped in warm pita. Crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, onions, and a generous drizzle of our signature garlic sauce. Simple, authentic, and always satisfying.

CHEESE SHAWARMA

Indulgent & melty. Our classic shawarma, upgraded. Loaded with a double portion of grilled meat, then layered with melted mozzarella and creamy feta cheese. Topped with fresh veggies and our special garlic-cheese sauce for an extra rich, gooey experience in every bite.

Spicy Shawarma

For the brave. Fiery marinated meat, sliced hot off the grill, combined with fresh veggies and a daring kick of homemade spicy harissa sauce. Finished with a touch of chili flakes and jalapeños for those who love their wrap with a blazing personality. Not for the faint of heart.
